Discover the story behind
DP JEWELLERS

"D.P. Jewellers" is the venture of D.P. Abhushan Ltd., a partnership firm converted into a limited company in 2017 and registered under the Companies Act, 2013.

The journey of D.P. Jewellers began in 1940 in Ratlam, Madhya Pradesh. Founded by Late Shri Dhulchand Ji Kataria during India’s independence struggle, he laid the foundation of the brand's legacy. His son, Late Shri Panna Lal Ji Kataria, carried it forward with purity and transparency at its core.

In 85+ years, D.P. Jewellers has grown into a retail chain across 10+ cities including Ratlam, Indore, Udaipur, and more — led by the Kataria family over generations.

The young generation continues the journey with dedication, trust, and honest business practices — values that define the brand today.
